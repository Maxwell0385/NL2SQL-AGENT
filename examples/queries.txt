# Example Natural Language Queries

## Basic Queries
1. Show me all customers
2. List all products
3. Display all orders

## Filtering
1. Show me all customers from New York
2. Find products in the Electronics category
3. List orders placed in January 2024

## Aggregation
1. How many customers do we have?
2. What is the average product price?
3. Total number of orders this year

## Complex Queries
1. Show me all customers from New York who spent more than $1000
2. What are the top 5 products by revenue?
3. List customers who ordered more than 2 times

## Joins
1. Show me all customers and their orders
2. Which products have been ordered the most?
3. Get customer names and their total spending

## Time-based
1. How many orders were placed in the last 7 days?
2. Show me orders from February 2024
3. When was the last order placed?
